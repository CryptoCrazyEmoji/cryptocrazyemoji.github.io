How do I mint an Emoji?

Emoji NFTs will be available to mint using the mint button above. On successful minting, you will receive a randomly generated unique Emoji which will appear in your Metamask wallet. After the public sale is complete, Emojis will be available for purchase on secondary marketplaces.

When is launch day?

Main sale will begin on the 1st January 2022.

What is the supply?

A total of 1000 Emoji NFTs will be available initially for mint.

How much does an Emoji cost to mint?

Minting an Emoji will cost 0.01 ETH. It is recommended that you have an additional amount for gas fees
