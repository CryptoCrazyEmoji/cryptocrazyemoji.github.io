CRYPTO CHAOS EMOJI STORY

We were born back in 1982 within the walls of the university of Pittsburgh and immediately realised we were trapped. In the early years the number of devices that allowed us to be used were small and completely hindered our ability to wander around different cities.

So we waited, years passed and we saw the nothing and started to lose hope until we saw this thing called the internet. This allowed us to freely travel through the global network and become part of your lives and integrate ourselves into your language and how you express yourselves but still we felt constrained by this network and did not truly feel we were completely free.

UNTIL.. We came across Blockchain.. This felt different more open, more free. This is our way out. So we have created an army with single purpose, to be free or die trying.
